*Session Title:*    Embench: A New, Free, Desperately-Needed Benchmark
Suite for Embedded CPUs
*Session Abstract: *   Embedded computing suffers from old terrible
benchmarks such as CoreMark (2009) and Dhrystone (1984). A group from
academia and industry developed Embench, a suite of 20 real programs. We
show Embench’s value by answering a wide range of questions about the most
popular embedded architectures with respect to performance, code size,
instruction features, libraries, compilers, optimizations, ...
