6. Embench 0.6
- --------------

At our last meeting we all took an action to consider new benchmarks
that could be included in a floating-point Embench suite.
- - BEEBS has some
- - we should increase the platform size, to say 1MB
- - can we add some machine-learning benchmarks.
- - should we use Cortex M4 or M7 as baseline.

In addition there was a desire for
- - easier configuration
- - extending the interrupt latency/context-switching benchmark
- - having a saner way of measuring code size!

Since that meeting, David Patterson has suggested some machine
learning benchmarks that are appropriate. This is an opportunity for
further discussion. In particular to find volunteers to lead
particular aspects of the effort.
